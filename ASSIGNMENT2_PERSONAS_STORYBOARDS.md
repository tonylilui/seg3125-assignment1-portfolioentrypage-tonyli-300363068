# PawCare Veterinary Clinic - Personas & Storyboards

## SEG3125 Assignment 2

---

## SERVICE BUSINESS SELECTED

**PawCare Veterinary Clinic** - A veterinary clinic that offers pet healthcare services by appointment.

---

## PERSONA 1: Sarah Chen

### Basic Information

- **Name:** Sarah Chen
- **Age:** 32
- **Occupation:** Marketing Manager at a tech company

### Intrinsic Characteristics

1. **Time-conscious and organized** - Manages multiple projects and priorities simultaneously
2. **Tech-savvy and digitally native** - Comfortable with online services and apps
3. **Pet-devoted** - Considers her Golden Retriever "Max" as a family member

### Relation to Technology

- **High tech comfort level** - Regularly uses mobile apps, booking systems, and online services
- **Prefers digital solutions** - Values convenience and ease of use
- **Quick decision-maker** - Wants to complete tasks efficiently

### Relation to Domain (Veterinary Services)

- **Experienced pet owner** - Has owned pets for 10+ years
- **Proactive about pet health** - Regular check-ups and preventive care
- **Budget-conscious but quality-focused** - Willing to pay for good service

### Goal

**Book a veterinary appointment for her dog Max's annual checkup within the week without calling, while viewing available time slots and service options.**

---

## PERSONA 2: Marcus Johnson

### Basic Information

- **Name:** Marcus Johnson
- **Age:** 58
- **Occupation:** Retired teacher

### Intrinsic Characteristics

1. **Cautious and detail-oriented** - Wants comprehensive information before making decisions
2. **Traditional but learning** - Prefers phone calls but is learning to use online services
3. **Pet care enthusiast** - Recently adopted two cats and wants to ensure they receive quality care

### Relation to Technology

- **Moderate tech comfort level** - Uses email and basic web browsing
- **Prefers clear instructions** - Appreciates step-by-step guidance
- **Slower decision-making process** - Wants to thoroughly understand options

### Relation to Domain (Veterinary Services)

- **New pet owner** - Recently adopted pets and wants to learn about care options
- **Research-focused** - Wants to understand different services and their purposes
- **Price-sensitive** - Wants to understand costs and value for money

### Goal

**Learn about the veterinary clinic's services, understand pricing for different procedures, and determine if this clinic is suitable for his newly adopted cats before making an appointment.**

---

## STORYBOARD 1: Sarah Chen's Journey (Modern Blue Design)

### Visual Theme

- **Primary Color:** Blue (#2563eb)
- **Secondary Color:** Light Gray (#f1f5f9)
- **Style:** Clean, modern, tech-forward
- **Typography:** Sans-serif, minimalist

### Storyboard Sequence

#### MOCKUP 1: Home Page with Quick Book Button

- Large hero image with banner: "Welcome to PawCare Clinic"
- Prominent "Book Appointment" call-to-action button
- Quick service overview with icons
- Contact information visible in header

#### MOCKUP 2: Service Selection & Calendar

- Simple service menu (Checkup, Vaccination, Grooming, Dental)
- Interactive calendar showing available dates for the week
- Time slot selection interface
- "Sarah" persona sees only available times for quick selection

#### MOCKUP 3: Appointment Confirmation

- Clean confirmation page with appointment details
- Pet name, date, time, service type clearly displayed
- Confirmation email sent notification
- Reminder: "Arrive 10 minutes early"
- Back home button

---

## STORYBOARD 2: Marcus Johnson's Journey (Warm Green Design)

### Visual Theme

- **Primary Color:** Green (#10b981)
- **Secondary Color:** Warm Beige (#f3f4f6)
- **Style:** Warm, informative, trust-focused
- **Typography:** Serif and sans-serif mix, traditional feel

### Storyboard Sequence

#### MOCKUP 1: Services Information Page

- Detailed service cards with descriptions
- Clear pricing for each service
- "Learn More" buttons for additional information
- FAQ section visible ("Why is dental cleaning important?")

#### MOCKUP 2: Service Details with Educational Content

- Expanded service description
- Benefits and what to expect
- Typical costs and duration
- Testimonials from other pet owners
- "Ready to book?" section at bottom

#### MOCKUP 3: Contact & Booking Confirmation

- Multiple contact options (phone, email, in-person)
- Reassurance text about clinic expertise
- Simple booking form after reviewing services
- Confirmation with detailed information packet link
- Contact information for follow-up questions

---

## DESIGN DIFFERENCES BETWEEN STORYBOARDS

### Storyboard 1 (Sarah Chen):

- **Focus:** Speed and efficiency
- **Layout:** Minimal information, quick actions
- **Interaction:** Direct path to booking
- **Visual:** Modern, minimal, sleek

### Storyboard 2 (Marcus Johnson):

- **Focus:** Information and education
- **Layout:** Comprehensive details, multiple options
- **Interaction:** Explore services before booking
- **Visual:** Warm, detailed, trust-building

---

## IMPLEMENTATION APPROACH

The final high-fidelity prototype combines elements from both storyboards:

- **Primary design:** Modern blue theme (Storyboard 1) for visual appeal
- **Functional elements:** Incorporates information depth (Storyboard 2) in Services page
- **Navigation:** Flexible to support both quick-bookers and researchers
- **Content:** Provides both quick actions and detailed information

This hybrid approach ensures the interface meets the needs of both persona types while maintaining a cohesive user experience.
